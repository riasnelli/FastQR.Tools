How does it work?

A quick guide to what QR codes are, how generators work, and tips to scan and share safely.

1) Choose a QR code type

Pick what you want to share—like a WiFi password, a website URL, a WhatsApp chat, or a location. Each QR type encodes a different kind of text.

2) Enter your details

Fill in the fields (for example SSID + password for WiFi). We format the QR payload using standard QR conventions so phones know what to do when scanning.

3) Generate and download

The QR code is generated in your browser and you can download it (PNG/SVG) to print, share, or add to signage.

Tips

  • Use a strong WiFi password—QR makes it easy to share without typing.
  • Print large enough so cameras can focus (especially for long content).
  • Test on iPhone + Android before you publish or print.
  • Keep it clean: don’t blur or stretch the QR image.
